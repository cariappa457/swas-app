You have zero network connection. Do not panic. Follow these tested precautionary actions to maximize your safety right now.

Safety Actions Checklist

OFFLINE EMERGENCY

ACT NOW

1

Scream "FIRE" not "Help"

People are more likely to respond to a fire alarm than a general cry for help. Be loud, clear, and unignorable.

2

Run to Crowded Areas

Do not hide in dark or isolated spots. Sprint towards the nearest well-lit street, store, or group of people.

3

Prepare Defense

Keep your keys between your fingers, grip your bag tightly, or pick up any heavy object nearby (rock, stick) to defend yourself.

4

Break the Distance

If someone grabs you, do not pull away—step into them, use elbows, target eyes, throat, and groin to force a release.
